KOA KAVA

01. Brief

Koa Kava is a company that sells kava root powder. They had an existing website but wanted to update a lot of what they had as well as add a few new products to the site. They wanted a clean, modern, and professional website that would appeal to their target audience. They also wanted a website that would be easy to navigate and would allow them to easily update their products and content.

02. Client Interview

We had a few phone calls about what the client wanted to change and add, after doing some research I brought up suggestions for adding new pages like a blog and an FAQ. She loved the idea and I started to create mockups using their same colors and fonts.
